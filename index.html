Turkey Goulash

Description

Turkey goulash is a hearty stew made with turkey meat, typically simmered with onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, and various spices, often served with noodles or rice. for more head to This website

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1 (14 ounce) can stewed, diced tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • Â½ teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta

Steps

  1. Step: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the turkey until browned.
  2. Step: Stir in the stewed tomatoes, garlic, tomato sauce, sugar and basil, and then simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Step: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, and then drain. Combine the pasta and turkey mixture; toss and serve.
